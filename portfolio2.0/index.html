About Me

The story so far

I'm Ayaad Ullah Baig — a first-year BS Artificial Intelligence student at SZABIST Islamabad, and a self-driven developer building real things beyond the classroom.

My academic focus spans object-oriented programming, digital logic design, computer networking, and C/C++ — the foundations that shape how I think about systems and code.

Outside of coursework, I design and build websites with a signature dark, cinematic visual identity — from folk music band sites to mountaineering agencies and streetwear outlets. I believe interfaces should feel as crafted as the ideas behind them.
